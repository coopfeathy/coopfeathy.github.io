Visual Hierarchy

Apple

www.apple.com

This website shows visual hierarchy because of its design colors and layout. They used a black background and a sleek picture on the homepage so you know exactly where to look. They also kept it simple by only having a menu and 2 other buttons to push so you know exacly what you want to push.

Hick’s Law

Google

www.google.com

This website shows hick’s law because even though google is a huge webpage its homepage breaks down the steps of searching and minimizes your choices by only having a search bar and a few other options like the menu.

White Space and Clean Design

Real Heroines

www.realheroines.com

This website shows white space and clean design by simply using white space and they keep their website simple and clean by only having circular pictures and names of people on the home page along with a menu.
