Target Audience:

The target audience will be people who are curious about the weather or who have specific questions about the weather.

Personas:

  1. A mom who wants to know the weather for her kids soccer game.
  2. A teenager who wants to know the temperature to know how many layers to where to school.

Scenarios

  • What is the temperature outisde today?
  • How fast is the wind blowing?
  • Will there be a storm tonight?
  • what is the humidity?
  • What time will the sun go down?
